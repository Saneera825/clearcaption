ClearCaptions is available to people with hearing loss at no cost

ClearCaptions is a Federal Communications Commission (FCC) - Certified telephone captioning provider. Our captioning service is paid through a fund established as part of the Telecommunications Relay Service in title 4 of The American Disabilities Act.

To qualify to receive a ClearCaptions phone at no cost you must provide certification from a qualified professional of your hearing loss.
