Requirement

ClearCaptions is only made available to individuals who have a medically recognized hearing disability necessitating their use of the service. To qualify for the ClearCaptions service you must have a medically recognized hearing disability that is certified by a qualified health professional.

To use ClearCaptions Ensemble, you will need:

  • Hearing loss necessitating your use of the service
  • High-speed Internet connection
  • Landline home phone service
  • ClearCaptions account for captions
